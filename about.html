About Us

BLACK SUEDE is an Indian company primarily committed to deliver Innovation and Convenience to its customers in every aspects, Therefore we are not only focused RETAIL and MASS- PRODUCTIONS, but also the one thing that is extremely difficult for every Brand/ Designer (ie) SAMPLING. WE HELP DESIGNER'S COVERT THEIR UNIQUE DESIGNS INTO PHYSICAL BEAUTIFUL FINISHED PRODUCTS. We are your one stop destination to all your leather needs spreading positivity and creativity with our unique finds. We are manufacturer of high quality of genuine leather goods like leather handbags, Men's Leather Bags, Woman's Leather Bags, Travel Bags, Leather backpack, Laptop Bags, office Bags, Messenger Bags, Hair On leather Bags, Duffel Bags, Fur Bags, Leather Hand Bags & Totes, clutches, ladies wallets, card holders & etc. What we can do for you? Would you like to sell high quality and commercial bags, but you don’t have the know how or designers in house to do it? We can help you! We look at your current collection and/or discuss your preferences and develop a collection especially for you. We design. develop and produce for you. Who we work for! We work for critical, high end customers with a sharp eye for quality, finish and style. Our customers know what they want, and we help them to get just that! Please don’t hesitate to contact us if you are interested or have questions. Our leather We use the best quality leather in order to achieve the look we want. Every option for leather gives a certain result in look and feel. We will advise you on how to get the maximum out of every foot of leather according to your preference. Connect with us on social media! Share your experience with us by sending a picture or video of yourself with the shiny new product you bought from us. We're always looking forward to reading your reviews and suggestions. Let's make it happen! HAVE ANY QUESTIONS? Contact us now using our contact page, or email us at blacksuedebombay@gmail.com
